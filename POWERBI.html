POWER BI

A specific POWER BI project from data sourcing and wrangling to analysis and visualizations

This report was done for friend, who is an Urban Planner, who asked me to look at the demographic changes in certain counties (8 counties in the Delaware Valley Region), and one township, for a project she was working on with AARP. AARP's membership is for people 50 years old and above. Most demographic data is looking at senior citizens which are usually defined as 65 and older and this was part of the challenge of this directive.

I started by finding data sources online, and I ended up using U.S. Census data in its raw form for the years 2011 and 2017. The first data document I downloaded had census data for all U.S. counties. Another set of data had Newtown Township demographic data in percentages of the total township population.

The bulk of this project was wrangling the data into a usable form, creating the Power BI data model and calculations for analytics, and then finally, creating the report.

The final data source model tables were created by extracting, parsing, standardizing, cleansing and consolidating the raw data. DAX calculations were used to convert some data into a usable form and to provide some simple but effective analysis of the demographic changes over time for the 50+ demographic.
